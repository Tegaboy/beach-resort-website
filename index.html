welcome to ocean reef beach resort

USA's biggest & finest tourist destination

Ocean Reef Beach Resort also known as Ocean Reef Bay is one of USA's finest and most interesting and spectacular tourist destinations. Ocean Reef Beach Resort is located in the river banks of Staten island, New York.

If you are a tourist looking for a mix of adventure and idyllic tranquillity, then Ocean Reef Beach Resort is the place to be. With over 65 acres of palm-fringed white sand beach, river and mangrove forest, our resort provides opportunities for tourists to observe at close quarters different species of plants and animals amongst which are palms, mangroves, epiphytes, monkeys, squirrels, mudskippers and birds such as bats, kingfishers, Seahawks, egrets and ducks.

Our natural ambience, warm hospitality and the luxurious comfort of our chalets are some of the unique features of our resort. Our spacious chalets offer a mix of archeological architecture and furnishings with all modern amenities you would expect in a 5-star resort.
